Our journey

Prosperity in any nation requires more than just resources, means, and potential. The mindset of society also plays a crucial role in a country's progress. People often fail to recognize the potential around them and in their respective countries and instead look abroad for employment opportunities and economic progress.

To address this issue and promote prosperity by leveraging available resources, 'RAW' has been conceptualized in Nepal. The show features entertaining games related to agriculture, entrepreneurship, and climate change, with the aim of developing new perspectives for prosperity through the mobilization of information, knowledge and various resources related to agricultural sector and economy of Nepal. The primary goal of 'RAW' is to promote awareness of agriculture's potential and significance in the economic and social growth of the country through an engaging blend of information, education, entertainment and themes related to agriculture, hence called ‘AGRITAINMENT’.
