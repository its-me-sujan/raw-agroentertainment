Meet our Team

The Revolutionary Agriculture War ‘RAW’ redefines agriculture through ‘AGRITAINMENT,’ blending entertainment with expert insights on sustainable practices. A visionary director, expert judges, and strategic leaders ensure seamless execution and impactful storytelling.

Shailesh Kandel Kshetri

Concept, Creator and Director

As the Director of the Agriculture Reality Show, Mr. Shailesh Kandel Kshetri brings over a decade of experience in media and film.

Sharad Chandra Shrestha

Senior Agriculture Expert

Mr. Sharad Chandra Shrestha is a former Secretary of the Government of Nepal with 32 years of experience in agriculture.

Sohan Babu Khatri

Head of Business Strategy

Mr. Sohan Babu Khatri, the strategic mind behind RAW’s initiatives, brings more than 15 years of experience as a corporate business consultant and educator.

Mritunjay Jha

Project Coordinator

Mr. Mritunjay Jha has amassed a diverse range of experiences in Development, Environmental Advocacy, Youth Employment, Project Management and Political Consulting.
